What is a Virus?

A virus is a piece of computer software written to do something bad to your computer. There are many types of virus that do a variety of different things to your computer but they are all malicious in intent. You can get a virus even if you have antivirus software installed but the chance of getting a computer virus is much lower. We recommend getting computer antivirus software if you do not have some already.

Common names

Since there are many types of programs that are designed to break into your computer here is a list of here is a list that includes some of the most known virus types and what they do:

  • Virus - This is the most general type of virus and all other types could call under this category. Some viruses could do multiple things.
  • Worm - A worm is just another type of virus
  • Keylogger - This is designed to capture everything you type on your computer, this could log and transmit your passwords.
  • Tracking Cookie - Tracking cookies are used by websites to see where you have been on the internet. They do not pose a threat to your computer but some antivirus programs may report and remove these.
  • Trojan - These try to get into your computer through security holes and will act like a general virus.
  • Spyware - This is software designed to track and spy on your activities. These could be in the form of toolbars for your browser.
  • Adware - This can be in the form of popups that display advertisements and can sometimes log what you are doing.
These are all different types of viruses. In the end they all are bad for your computer and should be removed and stopped from getting onto your computer.
