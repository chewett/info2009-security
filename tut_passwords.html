Do you know how to make a secure password?

Your password is the only thing between you and someone trying to hack into your account. For places like your bank account you should use a strong and different password from the ones you normally use. This means that if a third party service uses a bad system and gets hacked then you will not be at risk and they will only lose your weaker password.

A strong password:

  • Is longer than 10 characters e.g. thisisalongpassword
  • Hard is guess but something personal so you remember e.g. iliketoplaycomputergameslikemario
  • Has a mix of letters numbers and symbols, you could add a memorable number to the end of a good password and a random number of symbols e.g. passwordislong2011#####
For further information you could look at Microsoft's guide to creating strong passwords
