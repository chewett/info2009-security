What Antivirus?

Antivirus software is one of the most important pieces of software that you should be running on your computer. This software protects your computer by detecting and removing computer viruses. If you are not running some form of antivirus then you may be at risk of getting a virus.

Do I have Antivirus installed?

There are many types of antivirus programs on your computer, some of the most common are:

  • Norton Antivirus
  • Avast
  • AVG
  • ESET - Nod32
  • Microsoft Security Essentials
  • Kaspersky
  • McAfee

I don't have Antivirus, what do I do?

We suggest you install one of these antivirus programs, some of them cost money to purchase but others are free of charge. A good Free antivirus program is Microsoft Security Essentials. But any of the listed programs above should protect you well enough so you should be fine.

What else can I do?

Updating your software regularly is very important!
